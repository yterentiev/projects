Taco Trivia

When did the tacos first appear in the United States?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of the tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine )
